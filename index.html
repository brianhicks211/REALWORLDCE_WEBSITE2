About Real World CE

We are a technology-focused continuing education company that believes real-world experiences and scenarios should be incorporated into clinical education to improve not only patient care but also patient outcomes.

We are talented continuing education professionals with over 20 years of experience. Our team will design and deliver the highest level of clinical education.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide high-quality online programs and live meetings that incorporate our three primary principles — Real World, Science and Technology.

How we work

Real World CE employs educational experts with experience in developing and implementing a myriad of programs that are innovative, case-based, interactive and patient-centric in nature. We focus on applying adult learning theory and our core principles to every program. Even more importantly, we understand the nuances of our target audiences and design continuing education programs with a grounded understanding of how to educate both specialty audiences and general practitioners.
